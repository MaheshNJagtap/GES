Alumni : About Gurukul English School

The Gurukul English School (GES) was formed on 23rd June 2003 with the aim to build strong foundation of our nation by imparting good and qualitative education in the society. The alumni of a school are the symbol of its foundation and its expansion and the GES stands strong by its students coming out with flying colours. Students from five successful consecutive batches are excelling in different fields like Medical, Engineering, Social Services and many more.
